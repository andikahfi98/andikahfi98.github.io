ABOUT

Operations background, analyst mindset.

ADD PHOTO

Andi Muh. Alkahfi Samanglangi is a Data Analyst supporting B2B sales operations for more than 800 dealers at PT. Erajaya Swasembada, where daily and weekly reporting is automated across the whole dealer network.

Before analytics, he spent over three years as a Power Plant Control Room Operator at PT. Indonesian Tsingshan Stainless Steel — reading live production data, tracking electricity and coal consumption, and keeping distribution systems stable. That habit of watching a system closely and acting before small issues become big ones carries straight into how he approaches dashboards today: built for people who need to notice a problem the moment it shows up.

He now builds automated reporting, KPI monitoring systems, and performance dashboards using SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI — with a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and a Data Science & Machine Learning bootcamp from Purwadhika behind the switch.
