🌱 About

Andi Muh. Alkahfi Samanglangi is a Data Analyst supporting B2B sales operations for 800+ dealers at PT. Erajaya Swasembada, where daily and weekly reporting now runs on autopilot across the whole dealer network.

Before analytics, he spent three years as a Power Plant Control Room Operator — watching live production data, tracking electricity and coal use, and keeping distribution systems steady. That calm, watchful habit is exactly what he brings to dashboards today: built for people who need to notice something the moment it matters.

He builds automated reporting, KPI monitoring, and performance dashboards with SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI — backed by a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and a Data Science & Machine Learning bootcamp from Purwadhika.
