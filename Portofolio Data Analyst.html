About

A steady hand with data.

Andi Muh. Alkahfi Samanglangi supports B2B sales operations for 800+ dealers at PT. Erajaya Swasembada, where daily and weekly reporting now runs on autopilot across the whole network.

Before analytics, he spent three years as a Power Plant Control Room Operator — watching live production data and keeping distribution systems steady. That same watchful habit shows up in every dashboard he builds today.

He builds automated reporting and KPI monitoring with SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI — backed by a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and a Data Science & Machine Learning bootcamp from Purwadhika.
